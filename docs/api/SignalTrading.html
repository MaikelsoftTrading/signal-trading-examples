Structs

Candle

Represents price movement and volume within a specific time period.

IsolatedAccount

Account balances of a trading symbol which are updated by a signal when positions are opened and closed.

Ledger

Balances of the base asset or quote currency that are updated by the signal when positions are opened and closed.

Pricing

Last trade price, best buy price and best sell price at a given time. Signal needs these prices for detecting which price levels were hit (entry, profit target, loss limit) and for estimating the value of an open position.

Signal

Informs about the trading position that should be taken for a symbol. The Position of the signal can be either long, short or closed. The long and short trade setups of the signal define triggers for opening a position if Position is closed.

SignalPerformance

Equity and performance metrics of a trading signal.

SignalPosition

Represents the trading position of a Signal.

Symbol

Represents a trading symbol a.k.a. trading instrument at a broker or exchange.

TradeSetup

Specifies at which price to open a position, the size and leverage ratio of the position, as well as profit target and loss limit for the position.

Delegates

Strategy<TData>

The signature of a function that can set trade setups or change the Position of a signal, based on the latest analysis data.
